About Us

Discover our journey, mission, and vision

Our Story

Founded in 1910, ITC Limited is one of India's foremost private sector companies, with a market capitalization of over US$ 50 billion and a turnover of US$ 10 billion. ITC's businesses span Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Hotels, Paperboards & Packaging, Agri-Business, and Information Technology.

Our mission is to enhance shareholder value by creating world-class Indian enterprises that deliver superior and sustainable stakeholder value. Our vision is to be the most admired Indian enterprise, renowned for its commitment to sustainability and corporate social responsibility.

Sustainability

Our commitment to a greener future

Environmental Stewardship

ITC is committed to environmental sustainability through its Triple Bottom Line philosophy, which focuses on building economic, environmental, and social capital. Our sustainability initiatives are aimed at reducing our carbon footprint, promoting renewable energy, and ensuring sustainable sourcing.

We have achieved significant milestones in our sustainability journey, including:

  • Carbon positive for 16 consecutive years
  • Water positive for 18 consecutive years
  • Solid waste recycling positive for 13 consecutive years

Our Impact

Carbon Footprint: Reduced by 50% since 2005

Renewable Energy: 40% of total energy consumption

Water Conservation: 10 million liters saved annually
